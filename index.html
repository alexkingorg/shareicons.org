An Open Source icon to represent sharing on the web.

The Share Icon was created in December of 2006 by Alex King as an attempt to solve the problem of icon proliferation as social websites to began growing like weeds. The Share Icon is intended to be an abstraction for sharing to one or more social websites.

The Share Icon was released under the following four Open Source licenses:

  • GPL
  • LGPL
  • BSD
  • Creative Commons Attribution 2.5
